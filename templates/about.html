Making Essaouira Smarter

Our mission is to reduce traffic, minimize pollution, and make parking effortless for locals and tourists alike.

The Project

Smart Parking Finder is an intelligent web application designed to help drivers find real-time parking availability in urban areas. By utilizing real-time data processing, we connect drivers to open spots instantly near the Medina, the Port, and the Beach.

User Focused

Designed for simplicity and ease of use for all drivers. Save your vehicle, find a spot, and reserve it in seconds.

Eco Friendly

Less time driving around searching for parking means significantly lower CO2 emissions in the city.

Open Source Tech

Built using robust modern web technologies, Leaflet for dynamic maps, and a responsive frontend framework.
